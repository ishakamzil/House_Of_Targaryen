House Of Dragon

is an American television series created by Ryan Condal and George RR Martin . Prequel to Game of Thrones (2011-2019), is based in part on George RR Martin 's Fire and Blood . The series is set 200 years before the events of Game of Thrones and 172 years before the birth of Daenerys Targaryen . It describes the beginning of the demise of House Targaryen , the events that precede and cover the Targaryen Civil War , known as the "Dance of the Dragons". [1] [2]
